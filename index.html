// Wednesday Protocol + Daily System
RECOVERY SYSTEM
Golden Rule: You don't get faster in the gym — you get faster in bed. Training is the stimulus. Recovery is where adaptation happens.
🌅
Morning Sunlight
10 min outside within 30 min of waking. Sets your circadian rhythm, boosts morning cortisol (good cortisol — alertness), and improves sleep quality that night.
// 10 min daily
😴
Sleep 9–10 hrs
Growth hormone peaks during deep sleep — literally when muscles grow and CNS resets. Screens off 30 min before bed. Room cool (18°C) and dark.
// Non-negotiable at 14
💧
Hydration 3L/day
2% dehydration measurably reduces sprint performance. Urine should be pale yellow. Add electrolyte tablet on two-a-day days.
// All day, every day
🚿
Contrast Shower
2 min hot → 30 sec cold. Repeat 3 rounds, end cold. Promotes blood flow cycling and reduces DOMS. Not comfortable — that's the point.
// Post-training + Wednesday
🧘
Static Stretching
Hold each 45–60 sec. Priority: hip flexors, hamstrings, calves. Sprint speed is limited by how far your leg can extend behind you.
// Daily post-session
🛁
Epsom Salt Bath
400g salts, 15–20 min soak. Magnesium absorbs through skin — reduces muscle cramps and improves sleep quality. Do Sundays before Monday gym.
// Sunday + post-competition
🫁
Foam Rolling
Quads → IT band → calves → glutes → thoracic spine. 60 sec each, pause on tender spots. Avoid directly rolling joints or lower back.
// Post-training + Wednesday
📱
Visualisation
10 min Sunday: close eyes, run your perfect 100m and 200m in your head. Neural pathways fire the same as real running. Elite athletes do this.
// 10 min Sunday
WEDNESDAY HOUR-BY-HOUR
🌅
6:30 AM — Wake Slow
No alarm if possible. Natural light. Drink 500ml water immediately. Set the tone: calm, restoration day.
🥣
7:30 AM — Recovery Breakfast
High protein + anti-inflammatory. Smoked salmon, eggs, avocado, berries. Omega-3s are priority today.
🚶
After School — Easy Walk
20–30 min at a chatty pace. Below 120bpm. No sprinting, no gym. Active recovery promotes blood flow without fatigue.
🧘
30 min — Full Stretch
Hold every position 45–60 seconds. Hip flexors, hamstrings, calves, glutes, thoracic spine. No bouncing.
🛁
Contrast Shower
3 rounds: 2 min hot → 30 sec cold. End cold. Then foam roll 10 min before dinner.
🌙
9:00 PM — Bed Protocol
Screens off. Room cool + dark. Cottage cheese snack. Target 9–10 hrs. Tomorrow is a two-a-day — you need this.
